INITIATIVES

The Nature Conservancy (TNC) is a global environmental organization committed to conserving lands and waters for the benefit of people and nature. One of TNC's key initiatives is the protection of critical habitats around the world. Through land acquisition, conservation easements, and sustainable land management practices, TNC works to safeguard biodiversity and ensure the long-term health of ecosystems.

ACTION

TNC recognizes the importance of collaborating with government agencies to implement effective policies and practices for environmental conservation. One key aspect of TNC's involvement with governments is its participation in the development and advocacy of wildlife and habitat conservation policies.

ACTIVITIES

The Nature Conservancy (TNC) engages in a diverse range of activities aimed at conserving lands and waters globally. One of its primary activities involves acquiring and protecting critical habitats through strategies such as land purchases, conservation easements, and sustainable land management practices.
